Every Drop Tells a Story

In a small village in Ethiopia, a girl named Abeba used to walk hours each day just to collect water. That water wasn't always safe, and it often made her and her family sick. But thanks to donors like you, a well was built right in her village. Today, Abeba is in school full-time, her health has improved, and her future is brighter than ever. 100% of your donation goes directly to funding clean water projects—just like the one that changed Abeba's life. You're not just giving water, you're giving hope, opportunity, and life itself.

Our Impact

Since our founding in 2006, charity: water has funded over 64,000 water projects in 29 countries, providing clean water to more than 13 million people. Every project is fully tracked and reported back to you, so you can see the impact of your generosity.
